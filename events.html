Kurdy Biggs/Threedles

Nov 12

We were very lucky to have Kurdy Biggs of Threedles give a workshop for a new teaching design called Rescent. Over two days in May, 10 of us began stitching on this beautiful design, either in a blue or pink colorway... Shown here is the model from Kurdy that was finished as a tree topper. This is a teaching piece that will next be offered at the EGA National Seminar in Saint Louis.

About

Aloha and welcome to the website for the Aloha Chapter of the American Needlepoint Guild located on the island of Oahu in the state of Hawaii. Our meetings are held bimonthly on the 4th Tuesday of the month starting at 7PM. A stitch-in precedes each meeting, starting at 5:30PM. Meetings are held at Fiddlesticks Too, 1320 Hart Street, Honolulu, HI.
