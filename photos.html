Round Robin Sampler

In 2016, ten members of the chapter participated in a round robin sampler project. Each member selected a theme, a base thread or threads and stitched the first band before handing it off to the group, not to be seen again until each of the nine other stitchers designed and stitched a band following the color scheme and theme of the “owner”.
